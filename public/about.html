// About

Why this exists.

Dr. Gregory Lindstrom D.Eng. · CISSP · CISM
Houston, Texas

Thirty-five years in the field, one roadmap for students.

Dr. Gregory Lindstrom currently serves as Professor and Department Chair of Cybersecurity (Bachelor's) at Lone Star College. He has more than 35 years of experience in cybersecurity spanning military intelligence, NASA contracting, and senior enterprise leadership at global corporations including General Electric (GE) and Saudi Basic Industries Corporation (SABIC).

Dr. Lindstrom is also a professorial lecturer and doctoral advisor for the Online Engineering Programs at The George Washington University.

He holds a Doctor of Engineering in Cybersecurity Analytics from The George Washington University, an M.B.A. from the University at Albany, a Postgraduate Certificate in Cloud Computing from The University of Texas at Austin, and a B.S. in Computer Science from Athens State University.

The reason CyberRoadmap exists: I watch students navigate this field every semester. There's too much bad advice and not enough honest guidance — so this is the resource I wish they had.
