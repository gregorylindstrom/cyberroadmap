Certs, demystified.

Which cybersecurity certifications actually get you hired, which ones waste your money, and exactly what order to pursue them — from someone who's been in the field for 35+ years and watches students navigate this every semester.

Curated by Dr. Gregory A. Lindstrom · D.Eng. Cybersecurity (GWU) · Professor and Department Chair of Cybersecurity, Lone Star College · 35+ years: military, NASA, DoD contracting, CMMC consulting

The guiding principle

Match the cert to the actual hiring signal for the specific role and employer. Don't chase prestige. Don't follow generic progressions. Don't spend money to fix a problem you don't have yet.

Most students waste $1,000-3,000 on certifications that don't help them get hired — usually because someone on LinkedIn told them it was "required." What's actually required is the cert the employer's ATS is filtering résumés by. The list below is organized around that reality.
